Welcome to the Kleos Pad!

  • Play or pause the drum loop by pressing the play/pause button
  • Switch between different drum patterns by clicking the pattern buttons
  • Change speed of drums using the tempo slider
  • Click, hold and drag the mouse anywhere on the black background to change the filters on the drum sounds
  • No two locations will sound exactly the same
  • The audio filters also change the appearance of the drums!
